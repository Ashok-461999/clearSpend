Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Your data, your device, your control.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

MoneyMate is an offline-first application. All your financial data — expenses, income, budgets, goals, transactions, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any cloud servers, databases, or remote storage systems.

We never collect, transmit, or store your personal or financial information on any external server. Your data remains under your full control at all times.

No Data Collection

MoneyMate does not collect, track, or share any personal information, usage statistics, crash reports, or analytics data. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, tracking libraries, or advertising frameworks embedded in the app.

Internet Permissions

The app may request internet access solely for the following optional features:

  • Currency exchange rate updates (if enabled)
  • QR code scanning for UPI payments

No personal or financial data is transmitted during these operations. Exchange rates are fetched from public APIs without sending any identifying information.

Biometric Authentication

If you choose to enable biometric lock (fingerprint or face unlock), the authentication data is handled entirely by your device's native biometric API. MoneyMate never stores or transmits biometric data.

Data Backup & Export

MoneyMate provides CSV export and JSON backup features. These create local files on your device that you may choose to copy, share, or store elsewhere. Any data you share via these exports is your own responsibility.

We do not offer cloud backup. To protect your data, we recommend regularly exporting your data and storing it securely.

Your Responsibility

Since MoneyMate stores all data locally on your device:

  • You are solely responsible for backing up your data.
  • If you uninstall the app, all data will be erased.
  • If you lose or reset your device, data cannot be recovered.
  • We recommend exporting a backup before uninstalling or resetting.

Third-Party Services

MoneyMate does not integrate with any third-party services that process your financial data. The app operates entirely offline by default.

Children's Privacy

MoneyMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
